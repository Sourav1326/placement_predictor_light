Interview Tips
Do's
  • Research the company thoroughly
  • Practice STAR method for behavioral questions
  • Prepare specific examples from your experience
  • Ask thoughtful questions about the role
  • Maintain eye contact and confident body language
Don'ts
  • Don't speak negatively about previous experiences
  • Avoid giving generic answers
  • Don't interrupt the interviewer
  • Avoid asking about salary in first round
  • Don't show up unprepared for technical questions
